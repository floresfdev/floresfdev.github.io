How to provide remote software services

Abstract

In this talk we present the Tucma Software experience of providing remote software development and tech services from Argentina to Europe. This is the story of how we managed to create a remote business from scratch, the challenges in the operational, financial and technical sides and how to innovate by facing them in a culturally diverse environment.
